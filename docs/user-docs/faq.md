# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Is UniSync an official University of Cincinnati tool?
No. UniSync is a senior design project created by students and is not an official UC system.

## Does UniSync access my personal student data?
No. UniSync does not access or store any personal or private student data. It only uses publicly available information.

## Is my data stored when I ask questions?
User questions are not permanently stored or associated with individual identities.

## Can UniSync replace academic advisors or university offices?
No. UniSync is intended to supplement official resources, not replace advisors or administrative offices.

## What kind of questions is UniSync best at answering?
UniSync performs best with general questions about admissions, academics, and university services.

## Who built UniSync?
UniSync was developed by a team of University of Cincinnati students as part of a senior design capstone project.

## Will UniSync always be available?
Availability depends on the project development timeline and demonstration periods.
